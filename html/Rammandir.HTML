RAM MANDIR (AYODHYA)

Ram Mandir is located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh , It is the birth place of Shri Ram.
1>The temple is built in the traditional Nagara architectural style, standing about 161 feet tall over 2.7 acres of land.
2>It was officially opened for devotees on January 22, 2024.
3>The walls and pillars of the temple feature magnificent carvings and intricate artistic designs.
4>Every day, thousands of devotees visit this temple, making it a major spiritual and cultural center.
5>During diwali thousands of devotees gather near saryu ghat to lighten up the diyas and make world record.
