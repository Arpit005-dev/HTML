## Institute of Engineering & Technology, Ayodhya (IET Ayodhya) * The institute is part of Dr.RamManohar Lohia Avadh University (RMLAU) in Ayodhya (formerly Faizabad), Uttar Pradesh. ([rmlau.ac.in][1]) * Established around the year **2000-01**. The academic session started with two engineering branches (Information Technology & Computer Science) and an MCA programme. ([rmlau.ac.in][2]) * The engineering & technology expansion followed: ECE department in 2001-02, Mechanical Engineering in 2002-03, etc. ([rmlau.ac.in][2]) * It is recognised/approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
