MADHYAPRADESH (The Heart of India)

---->Madhya Pradesh, located in the heart of India, is the second-largest state by area and is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and biodiversity. Its name literally means "Central Province".

>The state occupies a central position in India, which gives it a wide variety of landscapes: forest and wildlife, rivers, hill-stations, as well as ancient heritage sites.
>MP has three UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the Khajuraho Group of Monuments, the Bhimbetka Rock Shelters and the Buddhist Monuments at Sanchi.
>The official tourism board emphasises heritage, wildlife, nature-drives, hill stations and spiritual tourism.
> Because of this diversity, MP appeals to several types of travellers: heritage & architecture enthusiasts, nature/wildlife seekers, spiritual travellers, and hill-station or monsoon break visitors.
